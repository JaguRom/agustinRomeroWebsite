The SideQuest app is a user-centered job search and hiring platform that aims to revolutionize the industry by putting the needs and desires of its users at the forefront. Through an extensive UX process, including the creation of user personas, empathy mapping, storytelling, flowcharts, benchmarking, and data analysis, the app was meticulously designed to provide a personalized and accessible job search experience. Its main features, such as geolocation-based job postings and community engagement, cater to the diverse needs of job seekers and employers alike. With a straightforward and intuitive interface, the app guides users through the job search process, ensuring a seamless and efficient experience. The implementation of a rating system further fosters trust and accountability within the user community. The SideQuest app stands as an MVP that combines innovative design thinking and data-driven insights to offer a user-friendly, reliable, and impactful solution for job seekers and employers in the ever-evolving job market.
